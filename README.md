What someone means and what they say are never quite the same.
What someone says and what the other person hears are never quite the same either.
This gap can't be closed, but it can be narrowed.

---

My goal is to narrow that gap so I can collaborate well and help make the work lighter.
Sharing my mental models with those I'm working with helps.
What's here is my best attempt at that: descriptions of how I think, not the thinking itself.

All models are wrong; some are useful. These are the ones I find useful today.

## What's here

### [Philosophy](philosophy/)

Philosophical models about collaboration.
Why the gap exists, what it means for alignment between collaborators, and how to work within it.

### [Concepts](concepts/)

Mental models for how I think and work.

### [Roles](roles/)

Personas I use in my work with AI agents, scoped to focused tasks like planning work, writing code, and reviewing changes.
